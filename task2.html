Bakhtiyor Zaynutdinov

About Bakhtiyor

Bakhtiyor Zaynutdinov (born 2 April 1998) is a Kazakh professional footballer who plays as a midfielder and forward. He is one of the most talented and versatile players of the Kazakhstan national football team.

Important Events

  1. 1998 – Born in Taldykorgan, Kazakhstan
  2. 2016 – Started his professional career with FC Taraz
  3. 2017 – Debuted for the Kazakhstan national team
  4. 2019 – Signed with FC CSKA Moscow
  5. 2023 – Continued career in Turkey with Besiktas

Key Achievements

  • Top player of the Kazakhstan national team
  • Known for his speed, dribbling, and versatility
  • Played in both midfield and attacking positions
  • Recognized as a leader of the new generation of Kazakh football
